Cultivation and Sourcing

Price: 0 SAR

At El Nile Herbs for Export, quality begins at the root. We cultivate our herbs in carefully selected Egyptian farms located in Fayoum and other fertile agricultural regions, ensuring naturally rich leaves full of aroma and flavor.

We collaborate with top farms that follow organic farming principles and adhere to global GAP standards. Our process focuses on choosing the right soil, using chemical-free natural seeds, and applying advanced agricultural practices that guarantee purity and quality from the earliest growth stages.

Every plant is cultivated under expert supervision to ensure healthy development, preparing it for the drying and processing stages with the highest quality possible.

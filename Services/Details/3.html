Quality Control and Testing

Price: 0 SAR

At El Nile Herbs for Export, quality is a commitment—not an option. Every production batch undergoes advanced laboratory testing to ensure the herbs are pure, clean, and fully compliant with European and international standards.

Our comprehensive testing includes:

  • Contaminant analysis

  • Pesticide residue testing

  • Moisture level measurement

  • Microbial and bacterial analysis

All tests are performed using state-of-the-art equipment under the supervision of specialized experts, ensuring that every product meets global export requirements.
Each shipment is accompanied by a Certificate of Analysis (COA) as proof of quality and transparency for our clients worldwide.

Drying and Processing

Price: 0 SAR

Drying is one of the most essential stages in herb production, as it determines the purity and overall quality of the final product. At El Nile Herbs for Export, we rely on a precise combination of natural and controlled industrial drying methods, following internationally approved standards to preserve the color, aroma, and essential oils of the herbs without loss.

We carefully regulate temperature and humidity throughout the drying process to ensure consistent, high-quality herbs that retain their natural properties and are fully suitable for use or export.
In addition, every production batch undergoes strict monitoring to maintain stable and reliable quality from start to finish.
